BTC Pricer - Show Bitcoin Prices on your Website

BTC Pricer allows you to update your website with bitcoin prices. Prices are calculated in real time from your local currency price and the current exchange rate from MtGox.

To use it, you need to:

  • Ensure that your page loads jQuery. For example, put this line in the <head> of your document: 
    <script src="http://ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/libs/jquery/1.9.1/jquery.min.js"></script>
  • Ensure that your page loads the convert2btc.js script: 
    <script src="http://www.btcpricer.com/convert2btc.js"></script>
  • Mark up your prices like this: 
    Item 1 price: $<span class="convert-usd-btc">23.99</span>
    You can also use any of the following currencies instead of usd: aud, cad, chf, cny, dkk, eur, gbp, hkd, jpy, nzd, pln, rub, sek, sgd, thb

You may want to download the convert2btc.js script and modify it to suit your needs, for example you might want to add in a discount or premium rather than just multiplying by the exchange rate. Or you might want to use a different format for the prices. The file's fairly small and simple so it should be fairly straightforward to modify it.
